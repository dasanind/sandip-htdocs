Welcome

In the Wireless Networks and Embedded Systems (WiNES) Laboratory at Northeastern University, we are designing a new generation of secure, reliable, energy-efficient wireless networked systems with higher intelligence and integration with the physical environment.

We focus on advancing the fundamental understanding of wireless networking. We also design and develop new networked systems, with a cross-layer and cross-disciplinary approach.

Please visit our research page for an overview of our ongoing research projects.






We gratefully acknowledge support from a variety of Federal and Private funding sources.
